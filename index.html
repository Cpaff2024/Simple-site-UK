About Us

At Simple Sites UK, we’re a small team dedicated to helping small businesses create an online presence that truly reflects who they are. We know that for many businesses, building a website can feel daunting, or something only larger companies can afford. That’s why we’re passionate about offering high-quality, affordable websites that give small businesses the opportunity to shine online.

Every site we create is unique, designed with your specific needs in mind. We focus on making things simple, user-friendly, and responsive across all devices—so your customers have a smooth, seamless experience, no matter how they find you online. From sleek, modern designs to functional features, we’re here to make your vision a reality, with plenty of care and attention to detail.

Whether you’re starting fresh or refreshing your current website, we’ll work closely with you to make sure you get the digital presence you deserve, without the big agency price tag. Your business is important to us, and we’re here to help you succeed online.
